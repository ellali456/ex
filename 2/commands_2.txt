* SeaBIOS - 
	* Basic BIOS implementation. 
	* open source

* running
	* after downloading all the neccesary packages, I compiled the seabios into a bios.bin product
	* qemu-system-x86_64 --boot menu=on,splash-time=15000 -bios ./out/bios.bin
		* menu=on - so i could enter the ipxe menu 
		* splash=time=15000 - so i would have time to press the manu option
		* ESC to enter BIOS menu 
		* ctrl B to enter ipxe menu 
			* dhcp 
				* in order to obtain an IP 
			* chain http://boot.ipxe.org/demo/boot.php
				* to boot with a basic linux image
